PEPITAS: What is it?
Project's Progress:
•PEPITAS is a project on a cryptocurrency system led by a team of 2nd year students at EPITA (see Team). The primary goal of this project is to discover and apply the fundamental concepts of a cryptocurrency and to implement them in C coding language. •Initially the project will aim to create a simple currency system, similar to Peercoin based on Proof of Stake (PoS) rather than Proof of Work (PoW), more generally used in this field. •The challenges and objectives of the project are not yet clearly defined, depending on its progress, certain additions could be implemented; the idea of a system of charitable donations for each transaction, in particular, has been mentioned and advanced on numerous occasions.
